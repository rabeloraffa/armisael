Raffael Candido Rabelo

Born and raised in Brazil, Raffael Candido came to U.S. to study Computer Engineering in a U.S. University. He was accepted into the Electrical and Computer Engineering degree at the New York Institute of Technology and he learned to love the EE subjects. Mostly interested in Communication Systems, he worked in different projects and engaged with many different student leadership activities. Raffael Candido took leadership in both Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineers and the National Society of Black Engineers. He was part of two projects: The Smart Bracelet: Armisael and the Autonomous Walker Project. His best technical skills are related to hardware design and development, control systems, mathematics and software design. His best non-technical skills are team leader, task oriented person, able to handle multi-tasks, always looking forward to learn more and have good communication skills.
