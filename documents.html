Demos

The first Demo is to illustrate how our communication system works. It shows the communication between 2 Bluetooth devices using two Arduni Uno board. This works the same way into our 3 Bluetooth communication system. You can see how each Bluetooth sends one data at the time: one sends and when it ends, the other sends and when it ends, the process repeats. Note: the character A is the character that indicates the end of the data being sent for Bluetooth 1; and B is the indicator for Bluetooth 2.

The second is the project's final version and it shows the combination of all the parts. You can see how the exoskeleton, after walking upstairs, increased the tension on the user's leg. This action guides the user to increate the speed or intensity of his activity. Similarly, if the heart rate is above the up target, the exoskeleton will relieve the tension on the leg.
